AI assigns themes to your responses

Consult uses artificial intelligence (AI) to assign each open-text response to one or more of your confirmed themes.

To make sure the data is reliable, consult adds 2 options to each question, to make sure no response is left out of your analysis.

The two added themes are:

  • Other = responses the AI found didn't fit any of your finalised themes, but that may still be relevant.
  • No Reason Given = responses the AI found that don't offer a viewpoint or information.

We start this step once your team has finalised themes for all questions.

This takes up to 48 hours, depending on the number of responses in your consultation. We will email you when the dashboard is ready for checking and review.

How the AI assigns themes

The AI reads each open-text response and tags it with every theme that applies.

A single response can have more than one theme. Longer answers often raise several points, so the AI doesn't force each response into one theme — it assigns all the themes that match.

Every response in your consultation stays available to view. The AI has coded them to help you choose which responses to explore, and from which angle, when you analyse your data.

Other

The 'Other' theme shows responses the AI found didn't fit any of your finalised themes, but that may still be relevant. This is where you might find a smaller theme worth exploring, or an outlier or individual viewpoint worth understanding. Other doesn't mean a response is irrelevant — only that it didn't align to the themes you chose.

No Reason Given

The 'No Reason Given' theme shows responses that don't offer a viewpoint or information. These may or may not still be relevant to understanding what your consultation is telling you. Other examples of responses are; 'no comment', 'as above' or an offensive response with no viewpoint.

Reviewing ‘Other’ and ‘No Reason Given’ at Anaylse phase is important. They may surface issues raised that your theme framework doesn't capture.
