Get started with Consult

Consult is an AI tool that helps policy teams analyse responses to open consultation questions faster and more consistently.

Consult is currently in pilot and relies on feedback to know where to improve.

How to use Consult

Consult phases:

  1. 1 Set up dataPrepare your consultation responses and get them ready to analyse. (6 steps)
  2. 2 Find Themes (AI)The AI suggests themes in your open-question responses, one question at a time.
  3. 3 Finalise your themesReview the AI's suggested themes and decide which to use to help your analysis. (6 steps)
  4. 4 Assign themes (AI)The AI assigns each response to your finalised themes.
  5. 5 Check qualitySee how the AI performed against a human benchmark. (6 steps)
  6. 6 Analyse in dashboardExplore your themed data and draw out what respondents said as part of your analysis. (6 steps)

What Consult does for you

Consult reads large volumes of responses, finds recurring themes, and assigns each response to a theme. It does what computers do best: spotting patterns across a lot of data.

That leaves you free to do what matters — working out what respondents have actually said, instead of sorting responses by hand.

You review the themes the AI suggests. You decide how respondents' views shape your policy recommendations.

Human judgement stays at the centre.

Scope of the current pilot

What Consult does:

  • Uses AI to analyse open survey responses. The AI finds themes in free-text answers but not in closed questions or from other sources (emails, pdfs, focus group notes etc). However, you can choose to manually add in these to match your survey data before putting data in to Consult.
  • Shows open and closed questions together in the dashboard, so you can use both in your analysis.
  • Generates themes one question at a time. It doesn't look for themes across questions.
  • Has checks to evaluate the AI's theme assignments for disagreements to maintain quality.

What Consult does not currently do:

  • Write a summary of your consultation. That's your analysis to make.
  • Judge whether a respondent supports or opposes an idea with sentiment. To capture that, ask it directly in a closed question, then look at it as part of your analysis.
  • Collect data — you'll need to use a survey tool to collect the data.
  • Give interim results before the consultation has closed or themes have been finalised.
  • Allow prompts that power Consult AI to be changed for an individual consultation if performance is not as expected.
